About Me

Hello, I'm Mark Y. Sumo, a Creative Graphic Designer, Website developer and an emerging Software Engineer.

Born and raised in Buchanan, Grand Bassa County, I am a graduate of the St. Peter Claver Catholic School and a Student at BlueCrest University Liberia majoring in Software Engineering. In addition to that, I am a graphic designer with years of experience utilizing industry-standard design tools like Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Indesign with the likes of other tools like Canva and Pixelabs to create visually appealing designs. I specialize in creating compelling graphics that tell stories and engage audiences. My passion lies in transforming concepts into beautiful, functional designs that make an impact.
